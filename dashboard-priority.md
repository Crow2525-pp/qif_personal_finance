# Monthly Review Dashboard Priority (Draft)

1 - Executive Financial Overview - AUD
   Primary executive snapshot of cash flow, savings, and health signals.
2 - Cash Flow Analysis
   Deep dive on inflows/outflows; validates the headline cash flow.
3 - Monthly Budget Summary
   Budget adherence and category pressure points for the month.
4 - Household Net Worth Analysis
   Confirms balance sheet movement that underpins executive KPIs.
5 - Savings Analysis
   Focused view on savings rate trends and drivers.
6 - Category Spending Analysis
   Broad category allocation and variance drivers.
7 - Expense Performance Analysis
   Identifies spending efficiency and outliers.
8 - Outflows Insights Dashboard
   Insights on outflows quality and concentration.
9 - Transaction Analysis
   Drilldown for anomalies/large transactions and categorization issues.
10 - Financial Reconciliation Dashboard
   Data quality and reconciliation sanity check.
11 - Account Performance
   Bank/account-level performance and balances.
12 - Financial Projections Dashboard
   Forward-looking outlook once current month is validated.
13 - Year-over-Year Financial Comparison
   Longitudinal comparison once monthly posture is clear.
14 - Four-Year Financial Comparison - Prior Year vs Previous 3
   Longer trend view; less critical for monthly cadence.
15 - Mortgage Payoff Dashboard
   Specialized; periodic check unless material change.
16 - Grocery Spending Analysis
   Niche category dashboard.
17 - Amazon Spending Analysis
   Niche merchant/category dashboard.
18 - Executive Overview (Mobile)
   Mobile-optimized executive view; secondary to desktop for review.
19 - Spending & Categories (Mobile)
   Mobile view; secondary to desktop category analysis.
20 - Cash Flow & Budget (Mobile)
   Mobile view; secondary to desktop cash flow/budget.
21 - Savings & Performance (Mobile)
   Mobile view; secondary to desktop savings analysis.
22 - Assets & Net Worth (Mobile)
   Mobile view; secondary to desktop net worth analysis.
23 - Projections & Analysis (Mobile)
   Mobile view; secondary to desktop projections.

Notes:
- Ordering prioritizes monthly review flow: executive summary → cash/budget → net worth → savings/spend → data quality → projections → long-term comparisons → niche categories → mobile mirrors.
- If you want a different cadence (weekly/quarterly), I can re-rank accordingly.
